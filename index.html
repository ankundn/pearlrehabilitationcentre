Pearl Rehabilitation Centre

A private psychriatric and psychological treatment centre for persons with pyschiatric illnesses, substance use orders and related challenges offering both in-patient and out-patient treatment programs

Vision

A society in which persons with mental health illnesses, substance use disordrs and related challenges have access to Quality Treatment Therapeutic Interventions.

Mission

To become a regional leader and partner in the provision of Quality Treatment and Therapeutic interventions to persons with psychriatric illnesses, substance use disorders and related challenges in Uganda and beyond.

Goal

To empower our clients so as to enable them to take control of their lives and accept responsibility for making changes that can lead to physical, psycological and spiritual healing.

Empower.Enable.Heal

Programs Offered

Psychriatric Assessment,Treatment and Referrals

Gathering information about a person within a psychiatric service, with the purpose of making a diagnosis.

Psychological Assessment

Understand an individual and provide valuable insights into the individual's behavior, skills, thoughts and personality.

Drug Screening Tests

Potential drugs are identified and optimized before selection of a candidate drug to test.

Community Based Rehabilitation(CBR)

Community action to ensure that people with disabilities have the same rights and opportunities as all other community members.

Advocacy

Public support for or recommendation of a particular cause or policy.

In-Patient Services

In-patient care requires overnight hospitalization. Patients must stay at the facility and during this time, they remain under the supervision of a nurse or doctor.

Out-Patient Services

Out-patient care doesn't require hospitalization. Patients can visit the facility for diagnosis or treatment.

Counselling

Provision of professional assistance and guidance in resolving personal or psychological problems.

On Admission

Visiting by registered sponsors is twice a month on Sundays between 11:00am to 5:00pm after the client has spent 14 days of admission.

A sponsor may leave pocket money with administration to cater for basic needs incase they get used up and any other such as haircuts or salon.

Personal requirements given on admission

  • Bed sheets, blanket, mosquito net and night wear.
  • Slippers(indoors), sports shoes and sports wear (co-curricular activities), open shoes and at least a pair of closed shoes for men.
  • Toiletries (bathing soap, washing soap, toilet paper, sponge, towel, non-alcoholic spray or deodrant/body cologne, body cream, hair brush, tooth brush, toothpaste, soap dish, detergent and a plastic shaver).
  • Wrist watch, Notebook and pen.

Dress Code

  • All clients should wear decently at all times ie. No outfits above the knees for ladies, no leggings and no netted clothing. All women should wear a bra and a petticoat when wearing dresses or skirts.
  • Men should wear loose fitting shirts/T-shirts and trousers.
  • No outfits with promotions or subtances should be worn at any time.
  • In case a client is found with any indescent cloth ot any unauthorised items, they will be confiscated.

Items not allowed on admission

  • Cars, Cameras, Laptops, iPods, iPads, Mobile Phones, Recorders, CD Players, Flash Disks, Scissors, Razor blades, knives and any sharp items.
  • All medication if any should be availed to the medical personel on duty (clients should not keep any medication).
  • No food items or drinks are allowed unless the medical personel on duty recommends.
